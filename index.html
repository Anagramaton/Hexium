  • 🔥 EMBER — Appears in the top row, and also appears whenever you submit a 6+ letter word. Extra embers can appear when you level up. Advances downward after each word. If it reaches the bottom — game over. Consume it in a word to destroy it. Using an Ember also rewards bonus gems equal to the word length bonus.
  • ✦ PRISM — Doubles the word score (×2).
  • ✦ RUNE — A wildcard. Tap it first to choose any letter, then chain it in your word.
  • ❋ DIGRAPH — A double-letter tile. Both letters contribute their point values. Counts as 2 letters toward word length.
  • ◈ AMETHYST — 🔮 A rare crystalline tile. Use it in a 5+ letter word to collect an Amethyst. Spend it to change any tile's letter on the board. Also advances downward each turn like an Ember — but if it reaches the bottom it converts to a random letter tile instead of ending the game.
  • ⇌ SELENITE — 🌙 A mystical moonstone tile. Use it in a 5+ letter word to collect a Selenite. Spend it to swap any two tiles on the board. Also advances downward each turn like an Ember — but if it reaches the bottom it converts to a random letter tile instead of ending the game.

ACHIEVEMENT TILES

These five rare tiles spawn as rewards for hard accomplishments. They display their actual letter value on the board. Use one in a 5+ letter word to collect it, then activate it from the power-up bar.

ORACLE
BEACON
ECLIPSE
LODESTONE

  • ⊙ ORACLE — Longest Word Hunter: unlocks when you submit a 9-letter word. Activate to highlight all tiles forming the longest possible word on the board for 5 seconds.
  • ◆ BEACON — Score Master: unlocks when you score 10,000+ points in a single word. Activate to reveal the highest-scoring word currently available on the board.
  • ☽ ECLIPSE — Portal Traveler: unlocks after using the portal in 3 different words. Activate to invert letter point values for your next word (common letters worth more, rare letters worth less).
  • ⬡ LODESTONE — Gem Collector: unlocks when you use 5 different gem types in one word. Activate to add +1 to the score multiplier bonus for your next word.
  • ∞ LEXICON — Endurance Master: unlocks after submitting 100 words in a session. One-time use — opens a panel showing the top 5 highest-scoring words on the current board.

Each achievement tile type can only have one active on the board at a time — once collected, it can spawn again.

After every 10 words submitted, two corner tiles transform into a portal pair — one entry (◈) and one exit (◉). Chain through both in the same word to link non-adjacent tiles. The portal closes after 5 words or when a portal tile is consumed.

  • ✨ XP — Every word earns XP equal to its word score ÷ 10 (min 1). XP accumulates across all sessions and never resets.
  • ⚡ Player Level — Levels 1–11 have fixed thresholds; beyond Level 11 each level requires +100,000 XP. Your level is shown in the top bar.
  • 💎 Tier Challenges — Tap your level badge to view SPARK → BLAZE → INFERNO → ASCENDANT challenges. Complete them for bonus XP.
  • ✦ Prism bonus — Prism tile doubles the final word score.
  • ◆ Gem stacking — Multiple gems of the same type are additive (3 Emeralds = ×6). Different gem types multiply together.

QUESTS & POWER-UPS

Open the 📋 Quests button to see today's challenges and this week's quest. Completing them earns bonus XP that is added to your total immediately.

  • 📅 Daily Quests — Three new quests each day (midnight UTC). Examples: submit 10 words, use 5 Ember tiles, score 10,000+ points.
  • 🗓 Weekly Quest — One harder quest per week (resets Monday UTC). Rewards up to 1,000 XP.
  • 🎁 Claiming rewards — Tap a completed quest item to claim its XP reward. Unclaimed rewards persist until you collect them.

Power-Up tiles give you active abilities:

  • ◈ Amethyst — Use it in a 5+ letter word to collect one. Then tap the Amethyst power-up button (top-left) and tap any tile on the board to change its letter.
  • ⇌ Selenite — Use it in a 5+ letter word to collect one. Then tap the Selenite power-up button (top-right) and tap two tiles to swap their positions.
  • Both power-up tiles also advance downward each turn like Embers — but reaching the bottom converts them to a regular letter tile instead of ending the game.

Achievement Power-Ups — five rare tiles that spawn only when you reach specific milestones. See the next page for details!
